Overview

The 2024 IEEE International Conference on Augmented Reality, Intelligent Systems, and Industrial Automation (ARIIA-2024) brought together leading researchers.

The International Conference on Augmented Reality, Intelligent Systems, and Industrial Automation (ARIIA-2026) serves as a pivotal gathering, bringing together leaders, researchers, and professionals from around the world. Its primary aim is to delve into and discuss the latest breakthroughs in these dynamic fields. Functioning as an esteemed platform, the conference facilitates the exchange of state-of-the-art research, innovative ideas, and pragmatic insights, significantly influencing the course of technology and industry. This event offers extensive networking opportunities, enabling participants, including researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders, to establish connections, foster collaboration, and form partnerships. Within this collaborative environment, there is a cultivation of idea exchange, discussions on emerging trends, and the exploration of potential collaborations that drive the development of innovations and their applications. This conference includes a wide scope, covering topics such as robotics and industrial automation, Industry 4.0 and 5.0 innovations, bioengineering and medical technologies, artificial intelligence, machine learning, communication, and networking. Attendees can expect to explore the latest developments in augmented reality, digital transformation, smart manufacturing, and much more. This conference serves as a platform for knowledge exchange, promoting discussions on the integration of technology into our daily lives and industries.
