Paper Submission

Academicians, researchers and industry people are invited to submit their original research articles, previously unpublished and not currently under review by another conference or journal.

Critical Information

  • Only Original Research Articles are accepted.
  • Please note that Review Articles are not accepted.
  • All submissions will be subject to a blind review process.
  • Acceptance will be based entirely on quality, relevance, and originality.
  • A minimum of one author of the accepted article must register for the conference and present the research paper.

Instructions for Submissions

  • The full paper should not exceed more than 10 Pages (including references).
  • Authors are requested to adhere to IEEE template provided below.
  • The similarity index (Plagiarism) should not exceed 15%.
  • All submissions must be uploaded in PDF format.
  • Do not combine the Full Length Paper and Similarity Report into one PDF.
