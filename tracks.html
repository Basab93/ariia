Conference Tracks

  • CAD and 3D Modelling
  • 3D Printing
  • Robotics
  • Robot planning, adaptation and learning
  • Internet of Robotic Things (IoRT)

  • Industrial Robotics
  • Humanoid Robot
  • Human–Robot Collaboration
  • Soft Robotics
  • AGV
  • Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)
  • Parallel and Serial Manipulator
  • Reconfigurable manipulators
  • Healthcare automation

Industry 4.0 and 5.0

  • Augmented Reality
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Digital Transformation
  • Human–Machine Collaboration
  • Digital Manufacturing
  • Digital Twin
  • e-Mobility and Additive Manufacturing
  • Collaborative Robots
  • Predictive Maintenance

  • Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

  • Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)
  • Smart Structures
  • Smart building and construction
  • Automation in agriculture and horticulture

Bioengineering and Medical Technologies

If your paper spans multiple areas, choose the closest primary track during submission; the program chairs may reassign for optimal review.

ARIIA

The International Conference on Augmented Reality, Intelligent Systems, and Industrial Automation (ARIIA-2026) is organised by Department of Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML), Dayananda Sagar university. The conference promotes exchange of ideas, research and industry collaboration.
